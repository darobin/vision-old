This document captures W3C's vision for action in the world.

It was created based on a brainstorming breakout during Team Day 2013. It is an evolving, unofficial, not-particularly-sanctioned, possibly-wrong-in-many-places write-up of our collective thinking.

The Universal Web

access for all, works for all, all can publish

builds a better world

Decentralised Control

democracy, fairness, interoperability, open markets, unified regulation

Better Web, Better World

the two are indistinguishable, privacy & safety everywhere, democracy, rationality, science, inclusive, universal community, a world of abundance

Actions

issue statements based on values, join (lead) internet protests when needed, do policy work, provide input to treaties, e.g. copyright & DRM
